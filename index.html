Brief Biography

I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at the University of California, San Diego under the supervision of Prof. Jorge Cortés. Prior to that, I recieved the B.Sc. (Hons.) degree and the M.E. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2018 and 2020, respectively, under the supervision of Prof. Ligang Wu.
